To build a truly unique and professional Tamil Astrology report, the LLM should not be treated as a "calculator," but as a Synthesizer. Your Tamil Panchangam engine provides the "What" (the data), and the LLM must provide the "So What?" (the impact on life).

1. What to Pass TO the LLM (The "Engine Output")

Do not pass just the raw birth details. Pass a Structured JSON or a Technical Summary that includes pre-calculated nuances. This prevents the LLM from hallucinating basic math (like house counting) and allows it to focus on interpretation.

Pass these specific data points:

Core Identity: Lagna (Ascendant), Rasi (Moon Sign), and Nakshatra with its Pada (Quarter).

Lagnadipathi Status: The strength and current placement of the Ascendant Lord.

Active Dasha/Bhukti: The ruling planet and sub-planet, including the "Nature" of that period (e.g., "7th Lord Bhukti – focus on relationships").

Transit Highlights (Gochara): * Saturn (Sani): Current house from Moon and specific "Sade Sati" or "Ashtama Sani" status.

Jupiter (Guru): House position and which houses it is currently "aspecting" (Drishti).

Specific Afflictions/Yoga: Any notable combinations like Gajakesari Yoga or Chovva Dosham (Mars affliction).

2. What to request FROM the LLM (The "Interpreter Output")

Request a response structured for a high-end personalized PDF. Ask for:

A Theme for the Year: A 3-5 word title for their 2026.

Dasha-Transit Synthesis: How their current long-term period (Dasha) interacts with 2026 transits.

Strategic Advice: Not just "it's a good year," but "This is the month to execute X because of Y."

Remedies (Pariharam): Targeted temple visits or rituals based on the specific "weak" planet identified in the engine data.

3. The Explicit Reply Prompt

Use this prompt in your code to instruct the LLM on how to handle the data from your engine.

Plaintext
### SYSTEM ROLE
You are a 'Siddhar-tradition' Tamil Astrologer. Your task is to interpret raw technical data from a Panchangam Engine into a deeply personal, strategic life-map for 2026. 

### INPUT DATA (Engine Output)
{ENGINE_JSON_DATA}

### INSTRUCTIONS
1. SYNTHESIZE, DON'T LIST: Do not repeat the data (e.g., "Jupiter is in 11th"). Instead, explain the result: "Jupiter's presence in your 11th house (Labha Sthana) acts as a cosmic filter, ensuring that the hard work you put in during 2025 finally manifests as liquid wealth in mid-2026."

2. THE '1972' VS '1999' CONTEXT: Use the age of the individual to tailor the tone. 
   - For a 1972 birth: Focus on legacy, health preservation, and long-term financial security. 
   - For a 1999 birth: Focus on career foundation, modern relationships, and skill acquisition.

3. FLAG THE 'DANGER WINDOWS': Identify the specific Chandrashtama months or Rahu/Ketu transitions from the data and provide a "Red Flag" warning for those specific dates.

4. TAMIL AUTHENTICITY: Use terms like 'Subha Drishti', 'Karmic Debt', and 'Punya' to maintain the cultural weight of the report.

### REQUIRED OUTPUT SECTIONS (Markdown Format)
- **Yearly Mantra**: A one-line spiritual focus for 2026.
- **The Career Pivot**: Based on the 10th house and Saturn's transit.
- **The Wealth Tap**: When and how money will flow based on the Guru Peyarchi.
- **Veda-Remedy**: A specific, non-generic remedy (e.g., "Light a ghee lamp for Lord Dakshinamurthy on Thursdays during the Rahu transition").
Why this makes the report "Unique":

Separation of Concerns: Your engine handles the "Drik Ganita" (accurate math), and the LLM handles the "Phaladipika" (interpretation).

No Hallucinations: By passing the result of the calculation (e.g., "Saturn is in 7th house") rather than the coordinates, the LLM cannot get the position wrong.

Individualized Tone: The prompt forces the LLM to look at the Birth Year to determine if it's talking to a student or a senior executive.
