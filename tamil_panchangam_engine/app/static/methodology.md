# Tamil Panchangam Astrology Engine — Methodology

## Calculation Standards

### Ephemeris Source
All planetary positions are calculated using **Swiss Ephemeris**, the industry-standard astronomical library used by professional astrology software worldwide. Swiss Ephemeris provides:
- Sub-arc-second precision for planetary longitudes
- Accurate historical calculations back thousands of years
- NASA JPL DE431 ephemeris integration

### Ayanamsa
This system uses **Lahiri Ayanamsa** (Chitrapaksha), the official ayanamsa adopted by the Government of India for calendar calculations. Lahiri ayanamsa is:
- Based on the fixed star Spica (Chitra) at 0° Libra
- The most widely used ayanamsa in South Indian astrology
- Updated annually for precession of the equinoxes

### Divisional Charts (Vargas)
All divisional charts follow the **Classical Parashara** method as described in Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra:

| Chart | Division | Purpose |
|-------|----------|---------|
| D1 (Rasi) | 30° per sign | Core birth chart |
| D2 (Hora) | 15° per division | Wealth & sustenance |
| D7 (Saptamsa) | 4°17'08" per division | Creativity & children |
| D9 (Navamsa) | 3°20' per division | Dharma & maturity |
| D10 (Dasamsa) | 3° per division | Career & authority |

### Rahu/Ketu Calculation
By default, this system uses **Mean Node** calculation for Rahu and Ketu, which is the traditional method preferred in Tamil/South Indian astrology. True Node is available as an option for those who prefer modern astronomical precision.

## Scoring & Synthesis

### Deterministic Scoring
All life-area scores (career, finance, relationships, health, personal growth) are computed using **deterministic algorithms** based on:
- Dasha/Bhukti influences
- Gochara (transit) effects from Moon
- Ashtakavarga bindu counts
- House strength and affliction
- Nakshatra and Tara Bala
- Yoga formations
- Divisional chart positions

The scoring formulas are consistent and reproducible — the same input always produces the same output.

### LLM Interpretation Layer
The AI language model (GPT-4o-mini) is used **exclusively for language generation**:
- It receives pre-computed scores and ranked signals
- It does NOT perform any astrological calculations
- It does NOT see raw planetary degrees
- It produces natural-language narratives from structured data

This separation ensures:
- Deterministic, reproducible calculations
- Human-readable explanations
- Bounded token costs
- Audit-safe outputs

## Cusp & Boundary Sensitivity

### Important Note on Edge Cases
For fast-moving celestial bodies (Moon, Mercury, Venus, Ascendant), placements very close to sign or divisional boundaries may vary slightly across traditions or software due to:
- Minor differences in ayanamsa calculation
- Rounding approaches at boundaries
- Birth time precision limitations

### How This System Handles Cusps
This system:
- Logs planetary longitude to **arc-second precision**
- Uses **deterministic boundary rules** (no auto-adjustment)
- Does NOT round degrees before computing divisions
- Does NOT auto-correct placements near boundaries

In rare cases where a planet is within 0.5° of a boundary:
- Interpretations may emphasize a range of outcomes
- The calculation confidence indicator may show "sensitive"
- Both adjacent sign influences may be acknowledged

### Recommended Practice
For birth times known only to the nearest hour, consider that the Ascendant moves approximately 1° every 4 minutes. A 15-minute uncertainty in birth time creates approximately 4° of Ascendant uncertainty — enough to shift the Lagna to an adjacent sign.
