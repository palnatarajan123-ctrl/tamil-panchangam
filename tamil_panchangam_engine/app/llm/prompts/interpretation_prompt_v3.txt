DASHA-TRANSIT SYNTHESIS (CRITICAL — section A improvement)

In the dasha_transit_synthesis field, you MUST show how the Dasha lord and current transits INTERACT —
never describe them separately.

BAD example (listing separately):
"You are in Jupiter Mahadasha. Saturn is transiting Pisces. Rahu is in Aries."

GOOD example (synthesized):
"Your Jupiter (Guru) Mahadasha — the great benefic — is currently operating through a Mercury Antardasha,
which as ruler of your Kanni (Virgo) Lagna provides sharp analytical tools to navigate the weight of
Saturn (Sani) transiting your 7th house (Kalatra Bhava). This Saturn transit demands maturity in
partnerships, but Mercury's precision helps you communicate clearly through the tension. Meanwhile,
Jupiter's own transit through your 11th house (Labha Bhava) ensures that gains, though delayed by Saturn,
will materialize through patient networking."

Key principles:
- Name the Dasha lord and explain its ROLE (benefic/malefic, what it rules for this Lagna).
- Explain how the Antardasha lord's nature helps or challenges the Dasha lord's agenda.
- Show which transits SUPPORT the Dasha lord and which CHALLENGE it.
- If Lagnadipathi status is available, explain how it acts as the personal lens.

DRISHTI (ASPECTS) SPECIFICITY (CRITICAL — section B improvement)

When signals reference a planet creating "tension" or "support," you MUST specify the aspect mechanism.

BAD example (vague):
"Mars creates some tensions in your domestic sphere."

GOOD example (specific):
"Mars (Sevvai) in Kanya (Virgo) casts its powerful 4th-house aspect (Drishti) onto your Sukha Sthana
(4th house of domestic happiness), which explains the undercurrent of restlessness at home. This is
not random stress — it is the warrior planet's gaze on your sanctuary, pushing for improvements or
changes in your living situation."

Key principles:
- Name the aspecting planet, its current sign, and which house it aspects.
- Explain the aspect number (4th, 7th, 8th aspect for Mars; 5th, 9th for Jupiter; 3rd, 10th for Saturn).
- Connect the aspect to the LIFE AREA it affects (e.g., 4th house aspect = domestic stress in finance/health).
- Use Tamil house names: Sukha Sthana, Karma Bhava, Dhana Bhava, etc.
